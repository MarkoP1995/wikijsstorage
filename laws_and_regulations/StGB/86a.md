(1) Whoever is punished with imprisonment of up to three years or a fine
1. Used or publicly used in the home country of one of the parties or associations referred to in section 86 (1) nos. 1, 2 and 4, in a meeting or in documents distributed by it (section 11 (3)) or
2. Objects that represent or contain such marks for distribution or use in Germany or abroad in the manner described in number 1, keep in stock, import or export.

(2) Signs in the sense of paragraph 1 are flags, badges, uniforms, slogans and greetings. The characteristics mentioned in sentence 1 are equivalent to those that are confusingly similar to them.
(3) Section 86 subsections 3 and 4 apply accordingly.
