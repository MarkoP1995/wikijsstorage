(1) A collecting society is an organization which is entitled by law or on the basis of a contractual agreement and whose exclusive or main purpose is to exercise copyrights or related rights for the collective benefit of several rightholders, regardless of whether it is in its own or in a third party Names.
(2) In order to be a collecting society, the organization must also meet at least one of the following conditions:
1. its shares are held by its members (ยง 7) or it is controlled by its members;
2. it is not geared towards making a profit.
