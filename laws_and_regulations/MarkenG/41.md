(1) If the application meets the application requirements and is not rejected in accordance with Section 37, the registered trademark will be entered in the register.
(2) The entry will be published. The publication can be in electronic form.
(3) For further processing or use for brand information purposes, the German Patent and Trademark Office can transmit the information entered in the register to third parties in electronic form. The transmission does not take place insofar as access is excluded according to ยง 62 paragraph 4.
